About Me

FinTech Leader · Researcher · Bridge Builder

Who I Am

I'm Eros Ye (叶明希), Operations Director of HSBC Technology China, overseeing the largest technology center in HSBC's global network — 18,000+ engineers across 4 cities (Guangzhou, Xi'an, Shanghai, Beijing).

Over 20 years in international banking technology, I've led cloud-native transformations, pioneered the industry's first financial-grade cloud implementation with Alibaba Cloud, built an Xi'an development center from 0 to 2,000 engineers, and cultivated an innovation culture that generates 30+ patents annually through an internal Angel Fund program.

I'm also a DBA candidate at City University of Hong Kong, researching how AI reshapes team dynamics and leadership. I hold an EMBA from CUHK Shenzhen and a Master's in Computer Science from SCUT.

What I Believe

🔹 Technology is the easy part. Culture, trust, and communication are where the real work happens.

🔹 AI is a teammate, not just a tool. The organizations that thrive will be those that learn to collaborate with AI, not just deploy it.

🔹 East and West have more to learn from each other than either side admits. Chinese speed meets Western rigor. Western creativity meets Chinese discipline. The synthesis is powerful.

🔹 Leadership is becoming more human, not less. In an AI-augmented world, the most valuable leadership traits are empathy, judgment, and the ability to hold complexity.

My Journey

Why This Platform

I built this platform for three reasons:

1. To share, not just consume. After years of reading others' insights, it's time to contribute my own — grounded in real experience running technology operations in China.

2. To bridge worlds. Too much English-language content about Chinese tech is written by outsiders. Too much Chinese content never reaches global audiences. I want to be a two-way bridge.

3. To build a body of work. Whether for future books, speaking engagements, or academic contributions — this platform is the foundation for everything to come.
