About / 关于

The Person Behind the Title

I'm the Operations Director of HSBC Technology China, overseeing a technology center with 18,000+ engineers across 4 cities — the largest in HSBC's global network. Beyond operations, I'm a DBA researcher at City University exploring how AI reshapes team dynamics and leadership.

With over 20 years in international banking technology, I've led cloud-native transformations, pioneered the industry's first financial-grade cloud implementation with Alibaba Cloud, built an Xi'an center from 0 to 2,000 engineers, and cultivated an innovation culture generating 30+ patents annually.

This platform is my way of bridging two worlds — sharing insights from China's FinTech frontline with a global audience, and bringing global perspectives back home.
