Research Focus

My research investigates the intersection of artificial intelligence, team dynamics, and cross-cultural leadership. As organizations increasingly deploy AI as collaborators rather than mere tools, traditional management theories need updating. My work proposes new frameworks for understanding how AI reshapes team formation, cohesion, and performance — particularly across Eastern and Western organizational contexts.
